Complexities of positive development

Allostatic load in children: The cost of empathic concern

Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 120(39), e2217769120

This study reveals how positive psychological characteristics, like empathic concern, moderate the relationship between environment and children’s well-being.

DOI: 10.1073/pnas.2217769120
Resilience
